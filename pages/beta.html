What is Peerio beta?

Tested and proven security. Your privacy, your way.

Why release software in beta?

Great development teams and programmers can design great software, but it's users who will make it perfect. You know better than anyone what you want from your messaging and cloud storage service. As Peerio continues to develop and innovate, your thoughts and ideas are shared directly with our developers to ensure Peerio is designed for you, by you.

Secure from the start.

Peerio builds off miniLock, an open-source, peer reviewed, and proven encryption standard developed by Peerio's lead software engineer and security expert. Peerio uses state-of-the-art encryption protocols that have been independently audited by expert cryptographers and system penetration testers to assure your data and privacy are protected.

A collaborative project.

When you trust a tool to protect you, you should be able to know how it works. Peerio's code is open-source and publicly available at GitHub for you to review and comment on. Peerio offers a bug bounty to encourage ongoing security research and reward those who dedicate their time to making Peerio as safe as possible.

Your ideas matter.

You don't need to be a programmer to have opinions about your privacy or the tools used to protect it. We want to know what you think about Peerio, online security, and what we can do to enhance the tools you use to maintain privacy online. Share your thoughts with us at feedback@peerio.com.
